Features

Everything You Need to Win at Money

One powerful app that replaces five separate tools — built for how Indians actually manage money.

🤖

AI Financial Brain

Powered by Claude AI. Get personalized spending insights, future expense predictions, and actionable savings recommendations every week.

📊

Real-Time Analytics

Beautiful Chart.js visualizations that update instantly — income vs expenses, category breakdowns, net worth trends, and more.

🏦

Bank Statement Import

Upload Excel or CSV bank statements. Auto-parses transactions and categorises them. Supports all major Indian banks including SBI, HDFC, ICICI.

🎯

Smart Budgeting

50/30/20 rule budgets, custom categories, live overspend alerts. See exactly where every rupee goes before it's too late to course-correct.

📈

Investment Portfolio

Track stocks, mutual funds, SIPs, FDs, gold, and crypto in one portfolio view. India-specific LTCG/STCG tax calculations included.

🔥

FIRE Calculator

Calculate your Financial Independence date. Adjust savings rate, expected returns, and inflation. See exactly how many years until you can retire.

🧾

GST & Tax Tools

Built-in GST calculator, annual tax estimator under old and new regime, and HRA exemption calculator — all in one tap.

Family Wallet

Manage joint expenses, split bills, and track shared savings goals with your family. Real-time sync across all family members.

🔐

PIN Lock & Security

Secure your financial data with a 4-digit PIN lock. All data encrypted locally. Zero data shared with third parties — ever.
